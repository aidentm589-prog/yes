What To Know

Pickup-first, gift-friendly, and easy to personalize.

When do pickups happen?

Subscribers receive pickup details by email during the first week of the month. If a guest signs up mid-month, the first pickup typically rolls into the next cycle unless they request otherwise.

Can the club be gifted?

Yes. The source site asks purchasers to provide the recipient’s name and email so Graze can send pickup details directly.

What about dietary restrictions?

Graze explicitly asks guests to note dietary restrictions at checkout so the monthly pairing can be adjusted when possible.
