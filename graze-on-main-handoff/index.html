The Graze Mood

A neighborhood wine bar with more depth than a plain order page can show.

Graze balances provisions-shop roots with the kind of room people want to settle into: grazing boards, strong glass pours, and a little more ceremony than the average night out.

Curated Wine Program

Organic, sustainable, biodynamic, low-sulfite, and small-production wines chosen to invite discovery instead of overwhelm.

Grazing + Small Plates

Custom charcuterie, rotating small plates, salads, and hand-stretched pizzas built for sharing with a bottle on the table.

Flexible Entertaining

From walk-in grazing to pre-ordered platters, wine club pickups, and guided tastings, the format flexes around how guests gather.

Cheese + Wine Club

A giftable ritual for people who like their evenings a little better poured.

Each monthly club box includes two bottles of wine, a cheese pairing, and grazing accompaniments. Pickup details go out during the first week of the month, and Graze asks guests to share any dietary restrictions at checkout.
